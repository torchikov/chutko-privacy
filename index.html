Sergei Torchikov ("Developer") built the Chutko app as a Free app. This page informs you of the policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the app.

1. Data Storage

All diary entries, emotions, body sensations, and reflections you create in the app are stored exclusively on your device. The Developer has no access to your personal entries. No data is uploaded to any server.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect photos, contacts, or other sensitive data.
  • We do not require account registration.

3. Analytics

The app uses TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as which screens are viewed and which features are used. TelemetryDeck is a privacy-first analytics service that:

  • Does not collect personally identifiable information.
  • Does not use cookies or device fingerprinting.
  • Does not track users across apps.
  • Is GDPR-compliant.

You can disable analytics at any time in the app's Settings.

For more information, see TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Export

The app allows you to export your entries as PDF or Markdown files. Exported files are created locally on your device, and sharing is handled through the standard iOS Share Sheet. The Developer does not have access to exported files.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is TelemetryDeck (described above). No other third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools are integrated into the app.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the app, please contact the Developer.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. You are advised to review this page periodically.
